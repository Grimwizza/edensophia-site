Hey, I’m Eden Sophia. Most days, I’ve got paint on my hands and my AirPods in. I literally can’t create anything without music playing. I love the feel of sketching in a real notebook, but I’m also constantly drawing in Procreate on my iPad.

I’m planning to study business in college and minor in art. When I’m not drawing or painting, I’m usually listening to records, building a Lego set, or working out. Right now, I’m just focused on practicing new techniques and seeing where my art can go. I’m excited to keep exploring different styles and learning as much as I can along the way.
